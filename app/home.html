Medication consistency is integral in ensuring long-term treatment success. Pill Buddy is here to help patients & caretakers succeed in developing & maintaining consistent care.

All caretakers & patients will have access to the notifications, patients, calendar, & settings tabs. Different functionality will be turned on & off depending on your subscription.

Notifications will give you live updates on missing data from patient profiles & will also sync with the calendar to provide real-time notifications to take medication. The patient tab allows you to view & edit all patient information around medication & scheduling whereas the settings tab is designed for you to update your account information.
